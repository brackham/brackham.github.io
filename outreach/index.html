Project POEM

Project POEM aims to foster interest in STEM careers among visually impaired middle and high school students. As part of the kick-off week for Project POEM at the UA SkyCenter atop Mt. Lemmon, I recently developed and led an activity that encouraged 12 Project POEM participants to explore the properties of transiting exoplanets using sonified transit light curves.
